More

November 17, 2002, in the vibrant city of Lucena, was the start of the life of Asher Gabriel R. Pantoja. His educational journey began at Aurora Elementary School in San Francisco, Quezon. After graduating elementary, he moved back in Lucena City, Quezon. At Manuel S. Enverga University Foundation, he finished his Junior High School and Senior High School education.

His passion lies in the realm of computers and applications. From fascination in games and being intrigued by how developers create intricate app designs, turned into a deep interest in designing innovative applications that simplify and enhance people's processes. He looks into user-friendly solutions to continuously explore new ways to make technology more accessible and beneficial for everyone.

He aspires to be a Front-end Developer to provide excellent application interface and experience that will be enjoyed by different kinds of users in the Philippines, and as well as the people of the world.
